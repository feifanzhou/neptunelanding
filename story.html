The Future of Creativity

Our Mission

Humans should not be machines. We live, breathe and come up with incredible new ideas and works of art. But too often our environment stifles our ability to do great things. Extensive red tape and greedy people trying to capitalize on every single thing someone else does are arresting progress in our world.

nepTune's mission is to liberate human creativity. We're creating a world where people are no longer content to push papers all day, a world of people who want to push boundaries. We're turning traditional business models inside-out and, with nepTune Music, we're creating an incredible community that supports musicians as they create amazing work.

Music is an art that touches everyone. For centuries, music evolved through many different styles, and all had their supporters. Talent came from around the world. But as the 20th century rolled along, a disease began to spread. Music became an "industry", dominated by "executives" and "celebrities". Artistry and nuance was stamped out by corporations who thought they knew what people wanted to listen to. We're creating nepTune Music to bring the art back into music. It's an incredible first step into the future of creativity—into the future of the world.

We envision a world where people are able to explore and create and make a career out of it. We envision a world where people can hear great music from around the globe, where artist deserve to make a living from their work. We envision a world where everyone can just be awesome.

Our Guidance

  • Design

    Software should never be difficult to use. It has to be beautiful and give you feedback. Like a good pair of headphones, great design is effortless and leaves you feeling happy. We continue to refine our design, making it cleaner and easier—because we use nepTune just as much as you do.

  • Transparency

    Industry and bureaucracy are characterized by thick, complex rules and regulations. nepTune has none of that. We're clear about everything we do, and you always know what you're getting.

  • Psychology

    We're fascinated by how people act. We want to discover the incentives that drive human behavior, because understanding what drives people is fundamental to understanding how to liberate human creativity.
